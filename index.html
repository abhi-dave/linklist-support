FAQ

1. How do I restore my saved links?
If iCloud Sync is enabled, your links will automatically restore when you reinstall the app or change devices.

2. I lost a category — can I get it back?
Yes, if iCloud Sync is on, your data should reappear automatically after re-signing in with the same Apple ID.

3. How can I suggest a feature?
Just email us — we’d love to hear your ideas!
