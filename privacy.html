Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

LinkList respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we handle it.

Information We Collect

LinkList does not collect, track, or share any personal data. All your saved links, categories, and notes are stored securely on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled).

iCloud Sync

If iCloud Sync is turned on, your data is stored in your private iCloud container and automatically synchronized across your Apple devices. We have no access to this data — it is encrypted and managed by Apple.

Analytics and Tracking

LinkList does not use any analytics SDKs, third-party trackers, or advertising tools. We do not collect usage statistics or personal identifiers.
